TOURNAMENT

The most exciting development in the world of esports motor racing is now in Auckland. Suit up VR gear and driving skills to the test and you could race all the way to the live Grand Finals at the 2018 Virtual 1 Auckland Championship in history as the first ever winner of the Virtual 1 Esports Series.

The Virtual 1 Auckland Championship 2018 is open to residents of all countries where the F1™ 2018 Game platform is available and who own a Xbox One, Xbox One S, Xbox One X, PS4, PS4 Pro or suitable Windows PC. Participants also require Xbox Live Gold Membership or PSN Membership with a suitable internet connection. Download the complete rulebook below.
