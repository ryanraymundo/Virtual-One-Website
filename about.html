ABOUT US

Virtual 1 is an International invitation to be held in a series of endurance eSport Formula 1 racing. Teams will compete for the fastest and adrenalin loaded virtual race event.

The inaugural season will kick off on October to together with the launch of the Virtual 1 Auckland Championship. With a qualification system running through the month globally, the Virtual 1 partnership around the globe determine the 50 quickest Formula 1 drivers (per competition category). They will be invited to the live semifinals, which will be held at the ASB showgrounds in Auckland on November 17. The top 25 from the semis will compete for the Grand Finals which will take place as part of the semi-finalist at same location on November 18, 2018.In the said dates, we will witness the first-ever virtual 1 eSport racing Auckland Championship.


To play the race at a gaming arcade is every child’s memorable experience. It is the excitement, the speed and the joy that thrills the player about competing for the race. It is for both young and old; and all genders who can relate to the sport in a digital platform. That is my inspiration behind this event, by bringing the excitement to the city and inspire each competing team. It is not only the thrill of the game but bringing as well a whole new level of virtual Formula 1 eSport racing
